Our Story

Built on trust, tradesmanship, and showing up on time.

ApexBuild started as a three-person framing crew in 2004. Today we're a full-service general contractor — but the job-site discipline that got us here hasn't changed.

Where We Started

From a single framing crew to a full-service contractor

ApexBuild was founded in 2004 by a small framing crew who got tired of watching good projects get mismanaged by contractors who never set foot on site. We started with houses. Word got around about crews who showed up when they said they would.

Two decades later, we've grown into a 85-person operation handling residential, commercial, and industrial work — but every project still gets the same dedicated supervisor and the same daily accountability that built our reputation in the first place.

2004

Founded as a framing crew

Three founders, one truck, and a handful of residential framing contracts.

2011

First commercial contract

Delivered our first retail build-out, expanding beyond residential work.

2017

In-house design & permitting

Brought architectural planning and permitting in-house to control timelines end-to-end.

2026

340+ projects delivered

Now an 85-person team serving residential, commercial, and industrial clients region-wide.
